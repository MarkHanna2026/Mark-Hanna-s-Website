About this page

This page focuses on my journey at UMass Boston, detailing the university's history and its IT tracks. I also share my background, achievements, future goals, and why I chose UMass Boston. It will inform you about me and UMass Boston in a nutshell. As well as it will have every link you need to help you navigate through UMass Boston, and all my social media links.

A little about me

My name is Mark Hanna. I am 23 years old and originally from Tanta, Egypt. I currently live in Fitchburg, Massachusetts. I moved to the U.S. in 2013, initially settling in Jersey City, New Jersey. In 2018, I relocated to Framingham, Massachusetts, where I completed my high school education. I then attended MassBay Community College, studying sociology and psychology. However, near the completion of my degree, I decided to switch to Information Technology. After exploring multiple universities, I chose UMass Boston.

About UMass Boston

Founded in 1964, UMass Boston is a public research university in Massachusetts. As part of the UMass system, it offers a diverse range of academic programs, emphasizing research, community engagement, and career preparation. UMass Boston ranks as the third most diverse university in the U.S., with a student population of approximately 15,000.

Why UMass Boston is for everyone

UMass Boston offers top-notch professors, affordable tuition, and numerous perks. The university provides hands-on learning opportunities and real-world projects. It also supports students with disabilities through programs like the Ross Center and offers free counseling for mental health. With over 215 clubs and organizations, UMass Boston ensures a welcoming and inclusive environment for all students.

UMass Boston IT Tracks

UMass Boston offers four IT tracks: System Administration, Information Architecture, Business Intelligence, and Computer Forensics. These tracks are available through the College of Management and the College of Science and Mathematics, providing flexibility for students interested in both technology and business.
