Why I Joined UMass Boston

I joined UMass Boston for a lot of reasons. First, the high-quality learning you get at UMass Boston. For example, hands-on projects, teamwork, labs, course materials, and professional professors using their real-world experience to implement their course material, which helps prepare the students. The college of management attracted me since I got to learn some business management and some Technology along with the track's choices. Which was my goal. The many sports programs and clubs that embrace any culture. Cost is one of the most important things because I come from the middle class, averaging 10,000 a semester, which is pretty good compared to other colleges. Convince in class time you have morning, afternoon, and evening classes along with Beacon flex, which offers either virtual or in-person class hosting. This is essential for me because I work full-time, and having many options like this is very convincing for me. Also, there are always events on campus which make it fun and interesting. Lastly, the counseling is free, which makes me feel comfortable finding resources on campus if I feel stressed or want to talk to someone, as well as it shows the university cares deeply about the students and their mental well-being. Overall, everything I want in a university, you, UMass Boston, got it.
